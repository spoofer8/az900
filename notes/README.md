## How to use these notes

1. **Read once, actively.** Every table, gotcha, and exam-hot box is placed intentionally. Read the whole file, don't skim.
2. **Test yourself.** Cover the right-hand column of any table and reproduce it from memory.
3. **Re-read on your review days** (Days 12–14 of the roadmap). By then you should be able to skim a file in under 5 minutes and know exactly what you don't yet own.
4. **Use `EXAM-CHEATSHEET.md` the night before** — it's the density-maximum distillation of everything below.

## Conventions

- **`Exam-hot`** boxes flag material that appears repeatedly in the exam bank.
- **`Gotcha`** boxes flag distractor traps — near-synonyms, deprecated names, common misconceptions.
- Every service is named with its official, current name (e.g. *Microsoft Entra ID*, not *Azure AD*).
- Numbers are current as of the 2024–2026 exam blueprint. Verify against the [official skills outline](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/credentials/certifications/exams/az-900/) before your exam.
